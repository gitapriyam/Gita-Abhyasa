Privacy Policy for Gita Abhyasa

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Gita Abhyasa is a Bhagavad Gita study app for reading chapters, slokas, meanings, and related audio or PDF resources. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Gita Abhyasa does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

App Content and Search Requests

The app connects to online services to load Bhagavad Gita content, search results, audio resources, and PDF resources. When you use search, the search text you enter is sent to the app's content service so that matching slokas can be returned.

Tracking and Analytics

Gita Abhyasa does not use third-party advertising, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not use analytics SDKs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. The app only sends network requests needed to provide app content and search functionality.

Children's Privacy

Gita Abhyasa does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.
